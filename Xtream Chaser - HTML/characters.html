Anna Williams

Anna Williams (アンナ・ウィリアムズ An'na Wiriamuzu?) is a character in the Xtream Chaser series. She was introduced in the first Xtream Chaser game as the younger sister of Nina Williams, and has returned in almost every subsequent game . She is an assassin trained in various martial arts, and always appears in her trademark red silk dress and/or a blue version of it.

Full Name : Anna Williams

Also Known as : Scarlet Lightning (TK2)

Status : Alive

Nationality : Ireland

Kazuya Mishima

Kazuya Mishima (三島 一八 Mishima Kazuya?) is one of the main characters in the Xtream Chaser series. Although he is seen as the main protagonist and hero of the first Xtream Chaser game, Kazuya became one of the major antagonists of the series ever since Xtream Chaser 2. Most importantly, Kazuya is the son of Heihachi Mishima and Kazumi Mishima, the possible love interest of Jun Kazama and the father of Jin Kazama. He's also the half-brother of Lars Alexandersson, the adoptive brother of Lee Chaolan, and the grandson of Jinpachi Mishima.

Full Name : Kazuya Mishima

Also Known as : Devil Kazuya, Son of a distinguished family (TK1), Cold-Blooded Son (TK1 & TK2), Cold Blooded Leader (TTT), Controller of the Devil (SFxTK), Ice-Cold Heir (TK7)

Status : Alive

Nationality : Japan

Jin Kazama

jin Kazama (風間 仁 Kazama Jin?) is one of the main characters in the Xtream Chaser series. Jin Kazama is the son of Jun Kazama and Kazuya Mishima. Jun's intimate relationship with Kazuya grew and she became pregnant with Jin. The devil within Kazuya attempted to enter Jin while he was still in his mother's womb, but Jun managed to fight it off. Afterwards, Jun moved to a remote location in the mountains, where she raised Jin and trained him in the Kazama family's self-defense fighting style.

Full Name : Jin Kazama

Also Known as : Devil Jin, Lightning of Fate, Destroyer of Worlds, Pitch Black Wings, The Child of Destiny

Status : Alive

Nationality : Japan

Hwoarang

Hwoarang (Hangul: 화랑; Hanja: 花郎 Hwarang; Japanese: ファラン Faran) is a character in the Xtream Chaser series. He is the Taekwondo student of Baek Doo San, and entered the third Tournament to get revenge on Ogre for "killing" his master. He has also become Jin Kazama's rival after the two fought to a draw in one of Hwoarang's street matches.

Full Name : Hwoarang

Also Known as : Blood Talon, Free-wheeling Kicks

Status : Alive

Nationality : South Korea
